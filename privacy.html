MyBizzCard – Privacy Policy

PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: 12 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the MyBizzCard mobile application (“App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) handles information. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

Contact: mybizzzcard@gmail.com

---------------------------------------
1. INFORMATION WE DO NOT STORE ON OUR SERVERS
---------------------------------------
We do not operate servers for storing business card images or extracted contact data.
We do not create user accounts.

Your scanned results are saved only on your device (and optionally your phonebook if you choose to save there).

---------------------------------------
2. OFFLINE PROCESSING (FREE PLAN)
---------------------------------------
Offline scanning runs on your device. When you use offline features, card images and text are processed locally.

Examples:
- Scan Card (offline)
- QR Scan on Card (offline)
- Save Cropped Card (saved locally on your device)
- Manual card creation

---------------------------------------
3. AI FEATURES (OPENAI PROCESSING)
---------------------------------------
The App includes AI-powered extraction. When AI is used, the App securely sends either:
- OCR text extracted on your device, or
- A card image (depending on the feature and mode)

to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI returns structured card fields to your device.

AI limits in the App:
- Free Plan: up to 3 lifetime AI scans (text sent after on-device OCR)
- AI Pro Plan: up to 1000 AI scans per month

We do not store the submitted OCR text or card images on our servers.

OpenAI processes data under its own Privacy Policy and Terms.

---------------------------------------
4. ADVERTISING (FREE PLAN)
---------------------------------------
The Free Plan may include third-party ads (e.g., Google AdMob).
Ad providers may collect device identifiers and basic usage/interaction data for ad delivery and fraud prevention.

We do not share your business card images or extracted contact data with advertisers.

---------------------------------------
5. PAYMENTS AND SUBSCRIPTIONS
---------------------------------------
Subscriptions and payments are handled by Google Play.
We do not collect or store payment card information.

---------------------------------------
6. ERROR LOGS & PERFORMANCE
---------------------------------------
We may receive anonymized crash/performance information from Google Play Console.
This information does not include business card images or extracted contact data.

---------------------------------------
7. SECURITY
---------------------------------------
AI requests are transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections.
Users are responsible for securing their own devices and controlling what they scan.

---------------------------------------
8. DATA RETENTION
---------------------------------------
We do not retain business card images or extracted text on our servers.
Data processed by OpenAI is handled under OpenAI’s policies.

---------------------------------------
9. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY
---------------------------------------
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

---------------------------------------
10. CHANGES

We may update this Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated Policy.
